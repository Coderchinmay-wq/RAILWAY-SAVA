Official safety guidelines from Indian Railways for a secure journey.

Passenger Safety Guidelines

  • Board/deboard only when train is stationary
  • Do not lean out of train doors/windows
  • Use overhead racks only for light luggage
  • Do not travel on train rooftops
  • Keep children away from train doors
  • Do not carry flammable materials

COVID-19 Precautions

  • Wear masks throughout the journey
  • Use hand sanitizer frequently
  • Maintain social distancing at stations
  • Carry digital copy of vaccination certificate
  • Use contactless payment methods
